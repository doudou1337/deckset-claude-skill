## Image Best Practices

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Use high-resolution images (1920x1080+)
- ‚úÖ Maintain consistent style
- ‚úÖ Optimize file sizes (keep under 5MB)
- ‚úÖ Use meaningful images that support your message

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use low-quality or pixelated images
- ‚ùå Mix incompatible styles
- ‚ùå Overload slides with too many images
- ‚ùå Use images just for decoration

^ Quality over quantity always
^ Every image should serve a purpose
